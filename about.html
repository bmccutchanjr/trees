Trees Puzzle Validation

This page is intended to assist designers of "trees" logic puzzles verify their puzzles. It has two main functions; validator and solver.

This application can be configured for one, two or three tree puzzles with 8 to 16 rows and columns.

Validator

Validator utilizes a brute force branching tree algorthm to find all solutions to a puzzle. No logical inferences are made about the puzzle or the puzzle configuration. Validator simply attempts to place a "tree" in every cell of the puzzle where a tree might ba allowed allowed according tree logic rules and the puzzle configuration. Once a tree is succesfully inserted, validator attempts to place a tree in the next cell.

Validator will continue in this fashion until the puzzle in solved or (more likely) the placement of trees produces an invalid solution. If a solution is invalid, validator abandons the current branc of the tree and continues from the last legally valid tree.

Validator looks at every possible configuration of trees and will find all (hopefully just one) solutions.

Validator can take a VERY long time to run.

Solver

Solver is used to demonstrate a puzzle can be solved withlogic alone. It uses the same logic a person might and attempts to solve the puzzle directly, applying them repeatedly until the puzzle is solved or it is unable to make additional progress.

Note that solver will always be a work in progress and may not be capable of solving every puzzle. New rules can be added, increasing solver's 'expertise'.
